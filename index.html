Draw Mouse click on the plot to draw
Pan Hold SHIFT and mouse drag inside the chart area
Zoom Use scroll wheel inside chart area

paintplot is a free online graphing application for drawing data series and plotting math functions.


Accepts valid math functions as defined in math.js

Examples:

x
abs(x)
x^3-4*x^2+20*x-9
sin(0.1*x)*cos(0.1*x)
2*e^(0.1*x)
rand(x)
rand(x)*sin(0.1*x)
