touchplot is a free online graphing application for drawing data series and plotting math functions.
Click on the graph to draw.

Accepts valid math functions as defined in math.js

Examples:

x
abs(x)
x^3-4*x^2+20*x-9
sin(0.1*x)*cos(0.1*x)
2*e^(0.1*x)
rand(x)
rand(x)*sin(0.1*x)
